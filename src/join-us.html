Sign-up below to get involved! Our current focus areas include:


  • Policy campaigns - Organizing around different tech-oriented legislative priorities and bills, from internet as a public utility to the NY Sustainable Data Centers Act
  • Political education - Educate fellow DSA members and New Yorkers about how technology touches all facets of our lives, from economic to housing policy
  • Tech worker organizing - Support existing tech unions and learn how you can organize your own workplace

  • Coalition building - Represent us in and outside of DSA spaces to communicate our priorities to other organizers and help building momentum around our campaigns
