Calendar Setup Required

To display your Google Calendar events, you'll need to:

  1. Make your Google Calendar public (or share it publicly)
  2. Get your Calendar ID from Google Calendar settings
  3. Add your Calendar ID to _config.yml as calendar_id: your-calendar-id-here

How to find your Calendar ID:

  1. Go to Google Calendar
  2. Click the three dots next to your calendar name
  3. Select "Settings and sharing"
  4. Scroll down to "Integrate calendar"
  5. Copy the "Calendar ID" (it will look like example@gmail.com or abc123@group.calendar.google.com)

Once you've added the Calendar ID to _config.yml, rebuild the site and the calendar will appear here.
